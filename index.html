What It Is

A revolutionary home bowling training system featuring real lane laminate, projected lane visuals, AI tracking for rev rates, ball speed, and a realistic throwing experience—all built for serious bowlers who want to train smarter at home.

StrikeBox™ bridges the gap between home practice and competitive performance. It gives bowlers at every level a reliable way to develop approach, timing, ball control, and tracking insights from the comfort of their space.

  • Real lane laminate surface for authentic feel
  • Smart projection-based pin simulation
  • AI camera system for ball rev rate, axis tilt, speed, and trajectory
  • App integration with data tracking and video playback
